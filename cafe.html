Your Favorite Cafe, Just a Click Away

Project Overview


The fan cafe website is the easiest way to order your favorite cafe meals. No complicated steps—just browse, order, and enjoy! Perfect for home-made meal seekers looking for fast, reliable delivery.

Features & Design


  • Simple Ordering: Choose your favorite items and place your order with ease.
  • Menu Variety: Explore a wide range of home-made meals to suit every mood.
  • Delivery to Your Doorstep: No need to leave your home or office; we’ll bring the cafe to you.
  • Tools used
    • HTML
    • CSS
    • JavaScript

How It Works


  • Access the site: Open the web site on your phone or computer.
  • Explore the Menu.
  • Call to order
  • Make payment for the meal
  • Sit Back and Relax
  • Pay the rider and enjoy your meal.
