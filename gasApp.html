Gas Delivery Web App

Convenient. Fast. Reliable.

About the App


The Gas Delivery Web App makes it effortless to order and receive gas wherever you are. Designed for speed and simplicity, this app ensures that you never run out of gas again. With just a few clicks, you can place your order and have it delivered straight to your doorstep.

Features & Design


  • Quick Ordering Process: Select your gas type and quantity, confirm your location, and place your order in seconds.
  • Real-Time Updates: Stay informed with live delivery tracking and instant notifications.
  • Customer Support: Access dedicated support for any issues or inquiries, available 24/7.
  • Tools used
    • HTML
    • CSS
    • JavaScript
    • Python Django

How It Works


  • Open the App: Access the web app on your phone or computer.
  • Choose Your Gas: Select the gas type and size that fits your needs.
  • Provide Your Details: Input your name, phone number & delivery address.
  • Place Your Order
  • Sit Back and Relax: Track your delivery and receive your gas on time.
