Bringing Clean Water, One Community at a Time

Project Overview


The Maji Ndogo Water Project is a 5-year initiative (2022â€“2027) aimed at improving access to clean water across underserved communities in Maji Ndogo. The dashboard tracks progress, evaluates impact, and highlights resource allocation to ensure transparency and efficiency in achieving water accessibility goals.

Features & Design


  • Interactive Filters: Navigate data by year, province, and water source type.
  • Visualizations:
    • Bar and pie charts to break down costs and water access by region.
    • Line graphs showing progress toward project completion over time.
    • Geographic map visualizations pinpointing water access improvements by location.
  • User-Centric Design: Clean, simple layout with color-coded metrics for easy readability.

Methodology


  • Data Collection
  • Tools Used:
    • Power BI for data modeling and visualization.
    • SQL for data extraction and transformation.
    • Excel for data validation and organization.
  • Approach:
    • Consolidated multi-source data into a centralized repository
    • Designed intuitive dashboards to provide actionable insights for stakeholders.

Results and Insights


  • Key Findings:
    • Over 50,000 lives improved in the first 3 years of implementation.
    • High-impact provinces identified, allowing for targeted resource allocation.
    • Effective reduction in project costs by 10% through optimization strategies.
  • Impact:
    • Enabled real-time tracking of water accessibility metrics.
    • Enhanced collaboration between stakeholders by providing a transparent view of progress.
