Pizza Sales Analysis Project

Uncovering Key Trends for Optimized Sales Performance

Project Overview


The Pizza Sales Analysis Dashboard is a dynamic tool designed to analyze the performance of pizza sales over a year. It provides actionable insights into revenue, ordering patterns, and product performance, enabling businesses to make data-driven decisions to boost profitability and customer satisfaction.

Features & Design


  • Interactive Metrics
  • Visualizations:
    • Daily and Monthly Trends.
    • Category and Size Breakdown.
    • Best and Worst Sellers Page.
  • User-Centric Design: Segmented views for peak times, product performance, and trends ensure clarity.

Methodology


  • Data Collection
  • Tools Used:
    • Power BI for data modeling and visualization.
    • SQL for data extraction and transformation.
  • Approach:
    • Transformed raw sales data into structured, insightful visuals.
    • Developed a dedicated page for best and worst sellers to optimize decision-making for inventory and marketing.

Results and Insights


  • Key Findings:
    • Peak Days: Thursdays, Fridays, and Saturdays drive the most orders.
    • Seasonal Trends: High sales volumes in July and January.
    • op Performers: Classic pizzas and large-sized pizzas are the most popular, with 14,888 and 45.89% of sales, respectively.
    • Underperformers: Identified low-selling categories and sizes for targeted improvements.
  • Impact:
    • Clear identification of best-selling and least-selling products aids in inventory and promotional strategies.
    • Enhanced understanding of sales patterns to optimize staffing and inventory.
